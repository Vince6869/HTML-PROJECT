Fun Things We Did in Palawan

  • Island Hopping: Explored the hidden gems and serene beaches of various islands.
  • Snorkeling: Discovered the vibrant marine life and colorful corals underwater.
  • Boat Picnic: Enjoyed a delightful meal on a traditional boat while surrounded by beautiful scenery.
  • Swimming in Hidden Lagoons: Relaxed in secluded lagoons surrounded by towering cliffs.
  • Cliff Diving: Jumped off cliffs into the refreshing waters for a thrilling experience.
  • Sunset Watching: Witnessed breathtaking sunsets over the ocean horizon.
  • Camping on Remote Beaches: Spent peaceful nights on remote beaches under the stars.
  • Fishing: Tried local fishing techniques and enjoyed the fresh catch.
  • Wildlife Spotting: Spotted unique birds, fish, and other wildlife native to Palawan.
  • Photography: Captured stunning landscapes and unforgettable moments.
  • Exploring Limestone Caves: Wandered through fascinating cave formations.
  • Shopping at Local Markets: Bought unique souvenirs and tried local snacks.
  • Beach Volleyball: Played fun games of volleyball with locals and friends.
